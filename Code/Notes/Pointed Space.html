The following are notes taken from the wikipedia entry on Pointed Spaces

A pointed space is a topological space with a distinguished point, the basepoint, that is picked out from spae and given a name, such as x_0. Maps of pointed spaces (based maps) are continuous maps preserving basepoints, such that f : (X, x_0) -> (Y, y_0). The class of all pointed spaces form a category Top. with basepoint preserving continuous maps as morphisms.
